Using a mix of qualitative data and user feedback, in 2020 we identified a handfull of issues with Discord's registration and onboarding. Applying max-width: 60ch is generally most effective for blocks of text, such as paragraphs, where controlling the line length directly impacts readability.

For headings, while it's also beneficial to maintain readability, they typically don't require the same constraints since they are shorter in length and serve as visual anchors rather than continuous reading material. However, ensuring headings do not span too wide a distance can still be important for overall layout consistency.
