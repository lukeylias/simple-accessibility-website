About

Who we are

Simple Accessibility is a space created for individuals who want to start creating accessible experiences. We noticed that many people feel overwhelmed and worried about getting started, so we set out to help people start their journey in accessibility.

You'll find:

  • Guidance covering various aspects of product creation from planning, design, writing, development, and testing.

  • Simple language that aims to help those unfamiliar with certian terminology.

  • General support for understanding and getting started with Web accessibility.

Who we aren't

We're not the one-stop shop for making your site fully accessible. While we're a good place to get started and learn the basics, there's so much amazing content already out there for those wanting to dive deeper. We felt we should focus on content for those early in their accessibility journey.

You wont find:

  • In-depth guidance for all accessibility aspects.

  • Full WCAG details for conformance.
