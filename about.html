About

Our mission

At Simple Accessibility, we believe that everyone deserves equal access to the web. But we know that getting started with web accessibility can be overwhelming. That’s why our mission is to simplify and lower the barrier of entry for anyone looking to make their website accessible.

What we do

We provide resources and tools to help you understand, implement, and test web accessibility. Our goal is to make accessibility easy to understand and simple to integrate into your workflow.
